Cost of Teeth Whitening in Nigeria

Purple Rain Dental Clinic · March 1, 2025

Teeth whitening is a popular way to improve the appearance of your smile. If you are considering whitening in Nigeria, you may wonder what it costs and what factors affect the price. Here is a simple overview from Purple Rain Dental Clinic.

Factors That Affect the Cost

The cost of teeth whitening in Nigeria can vary depending on:

  • Type of treatment: In-clinic (in-office) whitening is usually more expensive than take-home kits but often gives faster, more controlled results.
  • Product and technology: Clinics that use quality, safe products may charge more but offer better results and lower risk to your teeth and gums.
  • Location: Prices may differ between cities and clinics. We offer competitive pricing at our clinic.
  • Number of sessions: Some people need one session; others may need follow-up for desired shade.

Why Choose a Dental Clinic?

Having whitening done at a dental clinic ensures your mouth is healthy first (e.g. no cavities or gum disease that could be irritated). A dentist can also recommend the right option for you and reduce the risk of sensitivity or damage. Over-the-counter products may be cheaper but are often less effective and not tailored to your teeth.

Getting a Quote

To get an accurate cost for teeth whitening, it is best to have a quick consultation. At Purple Rain Dental Clinic we can assess your teeth, discuss your goals, and give you a clear price for the treatment we recommend.
